2. Typography Rules
Headline Font: Beatrice Display
Usage: Only for very short words or titles.
Rule: Minimum size 40px.
Character: Chunky, playful, and high contrast.
Body & Utility Font: Untitled Sans
Bold: Use for sub-headlines, highlights, and UI labels.
Regular: Use for all body copy and long descriptions.
Hierarchy: Clean, neutral, and highly readable.
3. Layout & Visual Logic (The "Lines System")
This is the brand's unique design signature:
Border Thickness: Layout lines must be 1/4 of the logo line size (typically 1px to 2px on web).
The "L" Frame: Use thin borders to create "framed" sections, inspired by the logo's bottom/right line system.
Minimalism: No heavy shadows. Use flat colors and thin lines to separate content.
Imagery: "Inside & Out" concept. Use window-like framing for photos. Portraits should be shot in natural daylight.
