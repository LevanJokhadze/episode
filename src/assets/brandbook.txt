EPISODE HOTELS - DIGITAL-FIRST BRANDBOOK V2

1) Core Palette
- Aqua Green (Primary Dark): #1F3436
- Calm Sky (Primary Light): #D1E9EC
- Dreamy Blue (Tech Accent): #B8DAF0
- Sweet Green (Status Accent): #D8F6C5
- Terracotta (Human Accent): #FED5CA

2) Interface Layer (Digital Skin)
- Deep glass overlays over photo/video backdrops.
- Primary panel style: backdrop blur + soft line + low-opacity glow.
- Pattern language: subtle grids, circuit-like connectors, scan lines.
- Avoid loud neon. Keep it premium, calm, and precise.

3) Typography System
- Display: Beatrice Display for emotional headlines.
- Body/UI: Untitled Sans for clarity.
- Technical Data: JetBrains Mono for stats and HUD labels.
- Hero headline minimum: 40px.

4) Motion Language
- Smooth momentum feeling on scroll and section reveal.
- Blur-to-focus image transitions.
- Drawn line/circuit transitions across sections.
- Haptic micro-hover: slight lift + glow (not bouncy).

5) UX Signature
- "Hotel OS" personality: service-first interface.
- Booking is a command center, not a plain form.
- Shared spaces communicate live energy (occupancy/vibe meters).
- AI Concierge stays always reachable.

6) Content Tone
- Intelligent, effortless, inviting.
- Human-first hospitality with data-assisted precision.
- Tagline direction: "More YOU." + "Intelligent Stay OS".

7) Product Baseline
- 118 rooms total.
- Types: Solo, Double, Twin, Triple, Spinner, Episode Special, Accessible.
- Highlight features: keyless entry, smart controls, luxury comfort.
